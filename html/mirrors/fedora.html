Fedora is a forward-looking Linux distribution that integrates the latest open-source technologies as they emerge. Sponsored by Red Hat and built by a passionate global community, it’s a proving ground for innovations that often make their way into enterprise systems. Whether you're a developer, sysadmin, or curious hacker, Fedora offers an upstream-first, polished experience built on speed, security, and standards.

It ships with GNOME by default, SELinux baked in, and benefits from close ties to major Linux developments. Fedora also supports multiple editions (Workstation, Server, IoT, Silverblue), making it versatile for all use cases.
